Final Space: Summary

Final Space is an animated science fiction television series created by Olan Rogers. The show follows the adventures of Gary Goodspeed, an astronaut who is serving a five-year sentence aboard the prison spaceship, Galaxy One. While on the ship, Gary befriends a strange, planet-destroying alien named Mooncake and together they embark on a journey to save the universe from the evil Lord Commander. Along the way, they are joined by a diverse cast of characters including an AI system named H.U.E, a rebel fighter named Quinn, and Gary's love interest, Little Cato. The series is known for its unique blend of humor, action, and heartwarming moments. The show explores themes of friendship, love, and the meaning of existence. The animation style of the show is distinctive and visually stunning, with its use of vibrant colors and detailed character designs. The soundtrack of the show is also noteworthy, featuring a mix of electronic, orchestral, and rock music. Final Space is a must-watch for fans of science fiction, animation, and adventure.

Gary Goodspeed

Gary Goodspeed is the main character of the animated science fiction television series "Final Space." He is an astronaut serving a five-year sentence aboard the prison spaceship, Galaxy One. Gary is depicted as a flawed but well-meaning individual who is seeking redemption for his past mistakes. He befriends a strange, planet-destroying alien named Mooncake and together they embark on a journey to save the universe from the evil Lord Commander. Throughout the series, Gary grows as a person and learns important lessons about friendship, love, and what it truly means to be a hero. He has a sarcastic and humorous personality, which is balanced by his bravery and selflessness when it comes to protecting those he cares about. Overall, Gary Goodspeed is a complex and compelling character who drives the action and themes of "Final Space."

Mooncake

Mooncake is a fictional character in the animated science fiction television series "Final Space." He is a small, round, and cute-looking alien who is initially believed to be a planet-destroying monster. However, as the series progresses, it becomes clear that Mooncake is actually a loyal and lovable creature who forms a close bond with the main character, Gary Goodspeed. Despite his small size, Mooncake is incredibly powerful and possesses the ability to destroy entire planets. Despite this, he is gentle and kind-hearted, and is fiercely dedicated to his friends. Mooncake becomes an important member of Gary's team as they journey through space, fighting to save the universe from the evil Lord Commander. He is known for his adorable appearance, quirky personality, and his catchphrase "Mooncake loves you." Overall, Mooncake is a beloved character in "Final Space" who adds humor and heart to the series.

Lord Commander

The Lord Commander is a fictional character in the animated science fiction television series "Final Space." He is the main antagonist of the show, depicted as a ruthless and powerful conqueror who is seeking to control the universe. The Lord Commander is depicted as a cold, calculating, and evil figure who will stop at nothing to achieve his goals. He is a formidable opponent, with a vast army at his disposal and the ability to command immense power. Throughout the series, the Lord Commander proves to be a formidable challenge for the main character, Gary Goodspeed, and his team as they try to save the universe from his clutches. Despite his evil ways, the Lord Commander is a complex character who is driven by a deep-seated sense of superiority and a desire for control. He is a formidable presence in the show and serves as a powerful force of opposition to Gary and his friends.
